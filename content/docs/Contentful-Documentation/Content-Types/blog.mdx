---
title: ✍️ Blog Post
description: Content structure for individual blog articles displayed on the blog page.
---

This content type defines a blog article to be displayed on the blog page.

---

## Fields

- **id** (`Text`)  
  Unique identifier for the blog post.  
  _Validation: must be unique._

- **title** (`Text`)  
  Title of the blog post.  
  _Validation: must be unique._

- **date** (`Date`)  
  Publication date of the post.

- **author** (`Text`)  
  Author name (optional).

- **category** (`Text`)  
  Post category (optional).

- **excerpt** (`Text`)  
  Short preview or summary of the article content.

- **content** (`RichText`)  
  Main article body.  
  _Validation: No marks or node types allowed (plain text only)._

- **image** (`Asset Link`)  
  Optional image associated with the blog post.
