## Fields

- **name** (`text`)  
  Name of the resource.

- **category** (`text`)  
  Classification of the resource.  
  _Allowed values:_  
  - Tips & Tricks  
  - Scholarship  
  - Research Opportunity  
  - Funds  
  - Education  
  - Career  
  - Learning  
  - Networking  
  - Other

- **description** (`rich text`)  
  A short description of the resource.  
  _Note: No marks or node types are allowed (plain rich text)._

- **link** (`text`)  
  URL to the resource.
