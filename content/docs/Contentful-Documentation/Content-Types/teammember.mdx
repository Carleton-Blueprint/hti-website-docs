Each entry represents a member of the HTI team, shown on the team page.

---

## Fields

- **name** (`text`)  
  Full name of the team member.

- **role** (`text`)  
  Position or title held by the team member.

- **bio** (`rich text`)  
  Short biography of the member.  
  _Max length: 400 characters._  
  _Note: No marks or node types allowed (plain rich text)._

- **specialties** (`array<text>`)  
  Optional list of skills or areas of expertise.

- **image** (`asset`)  
  Required image of the team member (profile photo).
